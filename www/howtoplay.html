— HOW TO PLAY —

Typy is a very simple game played by two (or potentially more) players. Here's what you need to know:

  • There are two text boxes, one above the other.
  • The text box at the top is yours, the one below it is your opponent's.
  • You can edit both text boxes and so can you're opponent.
  • The first person to type in the entire phrase correctly and hit the submit button wins!

Now that you've got a decent idea of how to play, its time to play for real! Click that button down there or click "Play" at the top of this page to start playing Typy.
